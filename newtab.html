Welcome to HomeBase!

This is your personalized homepage/newtab. Here's a quick guide on how to use it:

  • Add Tiles: Click on the "+" tile to add new websites.
  • Settings: Right-click on any empty space to open settings menu. customize your background, colors, fonts, and more.
  • Edit/Delete Tiles: Right-click on any tile in order to edit or delete it.
  • Create Folders: To create a folder, click and drag a tile over another tile. This will create a new folder.
  • Move Tiles: To rearrange the tiles, simply click and drag them to your desired position.
  • Add Tile to Folder: To add a tile to a folder, click and drag a tile over a folder.

Tip: Don't forget to export your homepage settings occasionally to have a backup of your links!

Pin the Extension to Your Toolbar

Pinning the extension makes it easier to add sites and access features quickly.

    Chrome

  1. Click the puzzle-piece (Extensions) icon to the right of the address bar.
  2. Find the extension and click the pin icon so it turns blue.

    Firefox

  1. Click the puzzle-piece (Extensions) icon to the right of the address bar.
  2. Find the extension and right click on it than click the pin to toolbar button.
